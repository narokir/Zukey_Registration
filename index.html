Zukey Registration

Prepare:

  • Username and password - Your Amazon username and password
  • Zukey - Get a Fathom 4 zukey from IT Hub
  • Temporary PIN - A temporaty PIN will be emailded to you at the time of registration.
  • Amazon laptop or desktop computer - A shared laptop is available at the IT Hub desk

Follow steps below to register zukey

1Ask IT to Pre-Login

2Sign in with your Amazon username and password

3Open Google Chrome and navigate to midway-auth.amazon.com

4Enter your Amazon username and Temporary PIN. Click “Sign In”

5Click “security key registration portal”

6Click “REGISTER SECURITY KEY”

7Click “Begin Registration”

8 Select "Fathom 4" Zukey type

There are several types of security keys at Amazon, but they all have the same features. ABQ2 is currently distributing Fantom4 USB-C Nano Zukeys.

9 Insert Zukey into USB-C port

Once the security key is inserted, the computer/laptop will take a few seconds to detect and identify the security device. Generally, you will see a notification about “new device” detection. If you don’t see a notification, check to make sure your device is properly inserted or reinsert the key into one of the other available USB ports.

10 Touch and hold inserted zukey for 3-5 seconds

Depending on the type of security key, you’ll need to apply light pressure to either its side or top. If you're pressing your security key but it is not entering a One-Time Password (OTP), remove your finger from the security key, click the OTP text box, and then touch the security key again.

Once the OTP is captured, you will automatically move on to the next step.

11 Create new PIN,and Click “Continue”

Must be at least 8 characters. Choose a security key PIN that's easy to remember. You’ll type it every day to log in to internal Amazon services and VPN.

12 Select checkbox labeled “DO NOT SCAN the QR CODE…” and click"Continue"

13 Click "OK"

14 Click "OK" to continue setup

15 Enter the PIN you created earlier and click "OK"

Enter the same PIN you created in step 10.

16 Touch and hold zukey for 3 seconds. Release then touch and hold again

Windows security will prompt you to touch the zukey. You may need to touch and let go twice. Watch the prompts as you touch they key.

Done!

If all steps have been completed correctly, you shoUld see a successful registration message.
