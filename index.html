Requests for Zukeys must be submitted through it-help.amazon.com and must be approved by L6 or above leadership.

Register your Zukey

Your Zukey is a physical or digital security device that provides an additional layer of security when logging into online accounts, applications, or services at Amazon.

Prepare:

  • Your Amazon username and password
  • Zukey
    Get one from IT Hub
  • Temporary PIN
    Will be emailed to you when you pick up your zookey.
  • Amazon-issued Windows laptop
    A dedicated security key registration laptop is available to you at the IT Hub

Where's my temporary PIN? Upon issuing your Zukey, IT will typically email you a temporary PIN for initial setup. Check your email for the temporary security key PIN.

Follow steps below:

1
Sign in to an Amazon laptop or computer with your username and password

Ask IT to Pri-login.

2
Open Google Chrome and navigate to midway-auth.amazon.com

The self-service key registraion laptop outside of the IT hub, has a shortcut to “Midway signin for zukey” on the desktop. Double-click the sortcut icon to go directly to midway authentication portal.

3
Enter your Amazon username and Temporary PIN. Click “Sign In”

Check your email for the temporary PIN.

4
Click “security key registration portal”

If you dont see this scren after sign in with your pin, go back to the previous step and enter your temp PIN again.

5
Click “REGISTER SECURITY KEY”

If you dont see this screen after sign in with your pin, go back to the previous step and enter your temp PIN again.

6
Click “Begin registration”

7
Click the image of the security key that looks like yours.

There are several types of security keys at Amazon, but they all have the same features. ABQ2 is currently distributing Fantom4 USB-C Nano Zukeys.

8
Insert your Zukey into USB-C port of laptop.

Once the security key is inserted, the computer/laptop will take a few seconds to detect and identify the security device. Generally, you will see a notification about “new device” detection. If you don’t see a notification, check to make sure your device is properly inserted or reinsert the key into one of the other available USB ports.

9
Click the One-Time Password(OTP) field

Depending on the type of security key you have, you’ll need to apply light pressure to either its side or top. If you're pressing your security key but it isn't entering a One-Time Password ( OTP), remove your finger from the security key, click the OTP text box, and then touch the security key again.

10
Touch and hold your inserted zukey for 3-5 seconds to generate One-Time Password(OTP)

Touch and hold the inserted security key to generate an OTP. Once the OTP is captured, you will automatically move on to the next step.

11
Enter a new PIN, confirm, and Click “Continue”

Must be at least 8 characters. Choose a security key PIN that's easy to remember. You’ll type it every day to log in to internal Amazon services and VPN.

12
Select “DO NOT SCAN the QR CODE…” checkbox and click"Continue"

Check the box labeld "DO NOT SCAN".

13
Click “Save another way”

14
Click “Use an external security key”

15
Click "OK"

16
Click "OK" to continue setup

17
Enter your new PIN from step 11. Confirm, and click"OK"

Enter the same PIN you created in step 11.

18
Touch and hold zukey for 3 seconds. Release then touch and hold again

When propted:

  1. Touch and hold your inserted zukey for 5 seconds
  2. Let go
  3. Touch and hold key again untill the browser refreshes.

All Done!
You have successfully registered your Zukey

If all steps have been completed correctly, you shold see a message about successful zukey registration. It may take upt tp one hour for your security key to work on al Amazon services and tools.
